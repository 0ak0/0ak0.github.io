At a Glance

I'm Oaken, a first class Game Design graduate. While knowledgable in programming, my main interest is in environmental and level design for virtual worlds. My work includes not just creating games, but adjusting them and their environments to properly adapt to what the player's needs are, such as in VR and XR applications. I also do simple 2D work, intended for use in texturing, logos, and UI elements.

Environmental Design

My work in Environmental Design includes not only creating levels and environments, but also modelling work and lighting. I have experience in creating environments tailored for XR use cases, taking into consideration the needs and requirements of such platforms. Concerning lighting, I use mixtures of diegetic and non-diegetic lights to not only make visible scenes, but capture the player's attention. I create scenes and environments with the goal of drawing player attention naturally and without the need for overly obvious markers or signals.
